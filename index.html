💡 Pro 3 Tips
🎯
Target zone6–9 bar peak · 30–45 sec total. That combination = balanced extraction on the Pro 3.
⚙
Grind is your main dialFiner = more resistance = higher pressure + longer time. Adjust 1–2 clicks at a time and log every shot.
🌡
Temperature by roastLight → 92–96°C · Medium → 90–93°C · Dark → 88–92°C.
💧
Pre-infusion5–10 sec at low pressure before ramping up. Reduces channeling and improves evenness on the Pro 3.
🫘
New bean?Start at your last known good grind for that roast level. The app will surface your good-shot zone once you log a few shots.
📋
Typical Pro 3 parametersDose 16–20g · Yield 30–40g · Ratio 1:1.8–1:2.5. WDT before tamping highly recommended.
